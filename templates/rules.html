How To Play
01

Take turns placing your symbol

Player X always goes first. Players alternate turns, clicking any empty cell on the 3×3 board to place their symbol — X or O.

02

Win by getting 3 in a row

Get three of your symbols in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally — and you win. Simple as the original game.

03

Watch the highlighted piece

When you have 3 pieces on the board, your oldest piece gets a coloured highlight — blue for X, red for O. This is a warning: it will vanish on your next move.

04

The 4th piece makes the 1st vanish

You can never have more than 3 pieces on the board. When you place your 4th piece, your very first piece disappears — freeing that cell for anyone to use.

⭐ Core Rule

The Vanish Rule

Each player can have at most 3 symbols on the board at once. When you place your 4th symbol, your oldest symbol is removed first — then the board is checked for a winner. This means the game never gets stuck, and strategy keeps shifting until the very last move.

Tips & Strategy
💡
The highlighted piece is your early warning system — plan your next move before it vanishes to avoid losing a good position.
🎯
The vanished cell is freed for anyone to use — watch your opponent's oldest piece too, it might open up a winning cell for you.
⚠️
On your 4th move onwards, the vanish always happens before the win check — so you can never win with 4 pieces on the board at once.
🔄
The game never truly gets stuck — because pieces keep disappearing, a winning move is always just around the corner.
